Our Story

Excellence in Polyester Engineering

Driven by a passion for technical excellence, Sparkit Knits Pvt. Ltd. has emerged as a specialist in premium polyester knitted fabrics. We focus on engineering high-performance POY (Partially Oriented Yarn) materials that deliver exceptional durability, moisture-wicking properties, and professional finishes. From our facility in Surat, we provide the global garment industry with versatile, high-quality knits designed for the modern world.
